<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center">ICAHN ENTERPRISES L.P. AND ICAHN ENTERPRISES HOLDINGS
L.P. ANNOUNCE RESULTS OF TENDER OFFER FOR UP TO 17,753,322 SHARES OF COMMON STOCK OF CVR ENERGY</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">SUNNY ISLES BEACH, Fla., January 9, 2025 &mdash;
Icahn Enterprises L.P. (NASDAQ: IEP) (&ldquo;IEP&rdquo;) and Icahn Enterprises Holdings L.P. (&ldquo;IEH&rdquo;) today announced the results
of IEH&rsquo;s cash tender offer for up to 17,753,322 shares of CVR Energy, Inc.&rsquo;s (NYSE: CVI) (&ldquo;CVR Energy&rdquo;) common
stock, par value $0.01 per share, at a price per share of $18.25 (the &ldquo;Offer&rdquo;), which expired at 5:00 p.m., New York City
time, on January 8, 2025.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Based on the count by Broadridge Corporate Issuer
Solutions, LLC, the depositary and paying agent for the Offer (the &ldquo;Depositary and Paying Agent&rdquo;), a total of approximately
878,212 shares were properly tendered and not properly withdrawn in the Offer.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">IEH will accept for payment all shares properly
tendered and not properly withdrawn at a price of $18.25 per share, for a total purchase price of approximately $16 million in the aggregate.
The shares to be accepted for payment by IEH represent in the aggregate approximately 0.9% of CVR Energy&rsquo;s outstanding common stock.
The Depositary and Paying Agent will promptly pay for the shares accepted for purchase by IEH.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">D.F. King &amp; Co., Inc. acted as information
agent for the Offer. Any questions regarding the Offer may be directed to the information agent toll free at (866) 207-3626 or via email
at CVREnergy@dfking.com</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: bold 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: bold 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">About CVR Energy</P>

<P STYLE="font: bold 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Headquartered in Sugar Land, Texas, CVR Energy
is a diversified holding company primarily engaged in the renewables, petroleum refining and marketing businesses as well as in the nitrogen
fertilizer manufacturing business through its interest in CVR Partners, LP. CVR Energy subsidiaries serve as the general partner and own
approximately 37% of the common units of CVR Partners, LP.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: bold 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">About IEP and IEH</P>

<P STYLE="font: bold 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Icahn Enterprises L.P. (NASDAQ: IEP), a master
limited partnership, is a diversified holding company owning subsidiaries currently engaged in the following continuing operating businesses:
Investment, Energy, Automotive,&nbsp;Food Packaging, Real Estate, Home Fashion and Pharma.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: justify">Icahn Enterprises Holdings L.P. (&ldquo;IEH&rdquo;)
is a Delaware limited partnership. IEP owns a 99% limited partner interest in IEH, and each of IEP and IEH are indirectly controlled by
Carl C. Icahn.</P>
